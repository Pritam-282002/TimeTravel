Enjoy a memorable day at the Taj Mahal in this private day trip from Delhi. Begin with a morning pick-up from your hotel in Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida or the Delhi Airport. Once you arrive at Agra, meet your tour guide at a local hotel. He will brief you on your upcoming sightseeing tour, you can refresh in the lobby bathrooms before embarking on your adventure. Begin at the Taj Mahal. No trip to Agra would be complete without a visit to this iconic building of domes and minarets. This tomb stands testimony to the timeless love story of the 17th century Mughal Emperor, Shah Jahan, and his wife, Mumtaz Mahal. Next, visit the imposing red sandstone Agra Fort (a UNESCO World Heritage Site). The elegant buildings inside reflect an interesting synthesis of Hindu and Central Asian architectural styles. The maze of the courtyards, mosques & private chambers of the fort echo the story of the Mughal Empire. You will then enjoy lunch at a local restaurant, followed by free time for shopping. Agra is known for its fabulous handicrafts made of marble and soft stone inlay work. Agra's major handicraft products besides inlay work are leatherware, brassware, carpets, jewellery and embroidery work. Afterwards, take time to take photos of the Taj Mahal from Mehtab Bagh. Also known as The Moonlight Garden, Mehtab Bagh is believed to have been designed specifically for the Shah to escape the crowds and see his great building in the moonlight. After a memorable day, travel back to Delhi. Return to your hotel in the evening.
